How to think about the total

Do not just ask, "How much is Germany?" Ask instead: which city, what room type, how often do you cook, and how much buffer do you want? That is how budgets stop being imaginary.

Big cities usually cost more than smaller student towns.
Shared housing can reduce the monthly pressure.
Your first month usually costs more than you expect.

Rent matters most

Food

Cooking helps

Leave emergency room in the budget
