Student Life in Germany

A useful site should explain everyday life, not just admissions. This page covers the real student mechanics: housing, transport, work, insurance, and the stuff that fills the calendar after the excitement wears off.

Everyday student life

Housing: dorms, shared flats, private rooms, and the hunt for a decent lease.
Transport: public transit is a big part of city life, so location matters.
Work: many students look for part-time work, but study balance comes first.
Insurance: keep your health coverage and paperwork organized.

What makes student life easier

A room close to transit or campus
A weekly food routine
Calendar reminders for deadlines
A few reliable friends and classmates

How the site should talk to students

Friendly, direct, and practical. Students do not need drama. They need direction, quick answers, and enough detail to make decisions without opening fifteen tabs and losing their will to live.
